The Challenge

Reliable wireless connectivity is essential for cloud gaming, immersive AR/VR, and real-time video communication. Yet at millimeter-wave frequencies, even minor environmental changes can cause sudden QoE degradation.

Digital Wave builds a vision-driven wireless digital twin using only commodity cameras enabling real-time diagnosis and mitigation of indoor mmWave impairments without new hardware.
